# CECREH Grant Strategy

Grant exploration and funding strategy for the **Center of Excellence in Capacity-building for Resilient Housing (CECREH)** at Texas Tech University.

## About CECREH

CECREH focuses on building capacity for resilient housing through research, technology, and community engagement. Key research areas include:

- **AI Disaster Agent** -- An AI-powered emergency guidance system for Texas residents facing natural disasters, featuring authority-grounded retrieval, NWS integration, and declarative governance by emergency managers
- **Gulf Hazard Interview** -- Qualitative research on Gulf Coast tenant/renter vulnerability, BERT-driven theme analysis, and renter-vs-homeowner comparative analyses
- **CENTAUR** -- Reproducible computational research workflow platform for academic research pipelines
