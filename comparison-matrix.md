## Key Distinctions

### By requirement type

| Dimension | Programs That Require It | Programs That Don't |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Community partner | S&CC, CIVIC, CHIRRP, FEMA BRIC*, GLO CDBG-DR* | HCC, ReDDDoT**, RAPID, DRMS, SAI, Gulf Research, Meadows |
| AI/computing focus | HCC, ReDDDoT | All others (general) |
| Disaster-specific | ReDDDoT, CHIRRP, RAPID, FEMA BRIC, GLO programs | HCC, S&CC, CIVIC, DRMS, SAI, Meadows, RWJF |
| Planning grant available | ReDDDoT, S&CC, CIVIC | HCC, CHIRRP***, RAPID, DRMS, SAI |
| Texas-specific | GLO programs, FEMA BRIC (through TDEM), Meadows, TX A&M | NSF programs, Gulf Research, EPA, RWJF, NOAA |

*Through TDEM or local government
**ReDDDoT requires a multi-sector team but not a traditional community partner
***CHIRRP uses EAGER mechanism which functions similarly to a planning grant

### By research vs. deployment emphasis

| Emphasis | Programs |
| --- | --- |
| Pure research | HCC, DRMS |
| Research + deployment | S&CC, ReDDDoT, CHIRRP, SAI, Gulf Research, NOAA |
| Deployment-first | CIVIC, FEMA BRIC, GLO programs |
| Data collection | RAPID, NIEHS R21 |
| Flexible | Meadows, RWJF, EPA, TX A&M/TDEM |
